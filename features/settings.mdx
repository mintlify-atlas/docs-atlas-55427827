---
title: "Settings"
description: "Customize your profile and appearance preferences."
---

The Settings page lets you update your display name, change your profile avatar, toggle dark mode, and delete your account.

## Account settings

### Change name

Click **Change name** to open a modal where you can update your display name.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the modal">
    Click **Change name** in the Account Settings section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your new name">
    Type your new display name in the **Username** field. The field cannot be empty.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save name** to apply the change. Your name updates across the app immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

To close without saving, click **Cancel** or press `Escape`.

### Change photo

Click **Change photo** to open an avatar picker modal.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the modal">
    Click **Change photo** in the Account Settings section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick an avatar">
    A grid of avatar options is displayed. These are DiceBear-generated avatars. Click any avatar to select it instantly — the modal closes and your profile photo updates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

To close without changing your avatar, click **Cancel** or press `Escape`.

## Appearance

### Dark mode

The **Dark Mode** toggle switches the app between light and dark themes. Click the toggle to switch; it turns blue when dark mode is active.

Your preference is saved automatically in `localStorage` and persists across sessions and page refreshes.

## Danger zone

### Delete account and data

The **Delete account and data** button is located in the **Danger Zone** section at the bottom of the page.

<Warning>
  Deleting your account is immediate and cannot be undone. Your session is removed from `localStorage` and you are logged out of the app.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Delete account and data">
    Scroll to the Danger Zone section and click the button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm in the dialog">
    A confirmation modal appears with the message "Are you sure you want to delete your account. This action cannot be undone."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm deletion">
    Click **Delete** to proceed. Your session data is cleared and you are redirected to the login screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

To cancel, click **Cancel** or press `Escape`.

## Logging out

You can log out from the sidebar navigation. Logging out ends your session without deleting your account or data.
